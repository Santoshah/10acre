Welcome to 10 Acre Ranch, an affordable drug rehab for men 18 and older, located in Southern California. We are fully licensed by the California Department of Health Care Services #330042BN. 10 Acre Ranch has been serving men struggling with addiction since 1992, and is a 501 (c) (3) non-profit agency. We accept most insurance policies and our private pay fees are economical and affordable.

LEARN ABOUT OUR PROGRAMS

Small-Scale Rehab, Big-Scale Recovery

Unlike most chemical dependency rehabilitation centers, our low cost drug and alcoholism treatment programs and long term residential rehab offer a warm, cheerful and homelike atmosphere in which men can experience a sober and drug free functioning lifestyle. They return to the mainstream as responsible alcohol and drug free individuals after 4 short weeks.

Our Southern California drug rehab facility has only 6 beds, 4 singles and 1 double. We are a small rehab by design so we can accommodate resident's therapeutic needs such as "one on one" counseling. Being a small drug rehab for men, we make sure individual needs are met and nobody slips through the cracks. We also schedule free time for recreation and fellowship, because at 10 Acre Ranch we insist on having some fun too!
